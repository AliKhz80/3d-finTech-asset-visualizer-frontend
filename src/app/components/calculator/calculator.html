info How to Calculate Money Exchange

This converter performs real-time margin exchanges by converting currency rates dynamically. To ensure accuracy across different markets, we employ a **USD Base Bridge** math model.

Step 1: Base Conversion (To USD)

The input currency (e.g. EUR or Gold Spot) is converted to its current equivalent value in US Dollars (USD).

Step 2: Quote Conversion (From USD)

The US Dollar base value is then converted into the destination currency (e.g. Gold Spot or EUR) using the target ratio.

All rates are automatically fetched from **AlphaVantage** digital indices. In the event of network rate limits, local cached telemetry will automatically be served as offline placeholders.
